Understand Risk vs. Reward
Higher potential returns typically come with higher risk. Understand your risk tolerance and create a balanced portfolio that aligns with your comfort level.

  • Moderate: Mix of stocks and bonds
  • Aggressive: Growth stocks, crypto

  • Stay invested during downturns
